“Kainang Pamilya Mahalaga Day” Unites Faculty, Staff, and Students of CTU - Danao

In a touching commemoration of the 31st National Family Week, the CTU Danao Campus of Cebu Technological University (CTU) united its faculty, staff, and students on September 29, 2023, to honor "Kainang Pamilya Mahalaga Day." Aligned with the directives of Presidential Proclamation No. 326, issued in 2012, this event served as a poignant reminder of the significance of nurturing family values within the academic community.
